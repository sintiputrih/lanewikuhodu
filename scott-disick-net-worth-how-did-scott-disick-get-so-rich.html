Scott Disick Net Worth: How did Scott Disick get so rich?

Scott Michael Disick is an American media personality and socialite.

Scott Disick was born on May 26, 1983 in Eastport, New York.

Scott Disick is most famous for starring as a main cast member on Keeping Up with the Kardashians and its spinoffs.

Scott Disick’s popularity on Keeping Up with the Kardashians led to the development of a house flipping show, Flip It Like Disick, that aired on E! Network.

Scott Disick Net Worth

Scott Disick has an estimated net worth of $50 million.

How did Scott Disick get so rich?

In addition to starring on reality shows, Scott Disick has pursued multiple business ventures, including starting a clothing brand Talentless, investing in nightclubs, and running multiple vitamin companies.

In 2010, when asked if he had a career, Scott Disick stated “I make a lot of money, and I’m more than capable of supporting myself. I run multiple companies in the vitamin world — QuickTrim, Rejuvacare, Monte Carlo Perpetual Tan.”

In May 2011, Scott Disick appeared on the cover of Men’s Fitness. A March 2015 episode of Keeping Up with the Kardashians revealed that Scott Disick was the President of Calabasas Luxury Cars, which is owned by his best friend and Flip It Like Disick costar, Benny Luciano.

Scott Disick often posts sponsored ads on Instagram, and he makes around $15,000 per post
