Most Expensive Lamborghini Models of All Time

The information & details has been compiled from reliable sites on the web such as Money Inc, James edition and Wikipedia.

20. Lamborghini Huracan STO

Price: $366,374

Debuted at the 2014 Geneva Motor Show, the rear-wheel-drive Huracan STO powers 631 hp.

Moreover, this model features a 5.2L naturally aspirated V10 engine, impressive aerodynamics, with an advanced track racing handling system.

In addition, the model’s body is rich with Alcantara and carbon skin (75% of the whole body) and a titanium stabilizer bar in its 4 point seat belts.

Further, the sports car can hit 62 mph (100km/h) in just 3 seconds. And has a top speed of 310 km/h.

This latest, fastest, and coolest Huracan’s racing model STO costs a whopping $366,374.

19. Lamborghini Diablo SE30

Price: $425,000

The precious and limited edition of Diablo is one of the 20 most expensive Lamborghini models of all time.

This rare model was first released in 1993 as a tribute car to the 30th anniversary of Lamborghini.

Moreover, this stunning metallic purple-painted beauty features a V12 60-degree engine, light aluminum alloys, an anti-roll bar, a free flow exhaust, and multiple fuel injections with the traction control system.

In addition, its 523 hp makes it hit the top speed of 333 km/h (207 mph).

Further, the most special and advanced edition of Diablo, SE30, costs a whopping $425,000.

18. Lamborghini Aventador LP 700-4 Miura Homage

Price: $427,820

On this spot, we have the ultimate predecessor of Lamborghini’s V-12 racing cars, the Miura Homage.

This special edition is a historical tribute car of the 50th anniversary of the Lamborghini Miura.

Moreover, Lamborghini Ad Personam created only 50 exclusive editions of this model in 2017.

In addition, this sports car features 18 metallic color variants, a 50th logo sewed on the top of the seats, matte silver and gold plating in its lower part, and a limited edition black plate on either side.

Further, Aventador Miura Homage costs a staggering $427,820.

17. Lamborghini Aventador S Roadster

Price: $460,427

On the seventeenth spot of the 20 most expensive Lamborghini models of all time, we have Aventador S Roadster.

At first, the model appeared at the 2017 Frankfurt International Motor Show.

The four-wheel-drive supercar features a 6.5 L V-12 engine, a 7-speed single-clutch ISR gearbox, four driving modes (Strata, Sport, Corsa, and Ego.)

Moreover, its 730 horsepower can hit a top speed of 217 mph and accelerate up to 62 mph in just 3 seconds.

In addition, the two-seater racing car includes two stunning convertible doors with a removable roof and the finest audiovisual and navigation features.

Further, this wild racing beast costs a staggering $460,427.

 16. Lamborghini Murcielago LP 670-4 Superveloce

Price: $496,902

Officially released in 2010, this model features a carbon fiber body, lightweight exhaust system, four rear-wheel drive systems, and a bigger air wing.

Besides, Lamborghini produced only 186 of these.

In addition, its 6.5 L V12 engine powers 670 hp and allows it to hit at a top speed of 342 km/h.

Moreover, this most ferocious, speedy, and powerful Murcielago edition costs a staggering $496,902.

15. Lamborghini Countach

Price: $544,322

Coming on the fifteenth spot of the 20 most expensive Lamborghini models of all time is Lamborghini Countach.

This classic late 80’s sports car comprises the unheard iconic wedge style designs and a rear mid-engine.

Similarly, Lamborghini produced only 1,983 Countach from 1974-1990 when the press covers went crazy over unseen beauty.

Moreover, Countach was the most powerful and the fastest sports car in the market during its origin. It uses a 5.2 L V-12 engine producing about 450 hp and 370-foot pounds of torque.

With a total speed of 183 mph and acceleration capacity of 0-60 mph in just 4.7 seconds, Countach was a strictly illegal and insane race car ever made during its era.

In addition, this supercar includes a multi-tubular frame underneath its body, 80s pop-up headlights, scissor doors, and a manual fast-speed transmission with reverse lockout.

Further, the price tag of this insane, wild, and aggressive-looking Countach is a staggering $544,322.

14. Aventador LP 720-4

Price: $548,000

A 50th Anniversary tribute car of Lamborghini, Aventador LP 720-4 is the fourteenth most expensive Lamborghini model of all time.

Similarly, this model features a dual-mode exhaust, electro-hydraulic power steering, exclusive aero packages, an all-wheel-drive system, an ISR gearbox, and three driving modes (Strata, Sport, Corsa).

Moreover, Lamborghini produced only 100 units of this eye-catching model worldwide.

In addition, this model produces 720 brake horsepower and costs a staggering $548,000.

13. Aventador LP 770-4 SVJ

Price: $573,966

Aventador LP 770-4 SVJ is a 2019 fifth version of Lamborghini Aventador.

Besides, the sports car comprises a mid-engine V12, four-wheel driving and steering system, a light carbon body with a 759 hp, and a top speed of 217 mph.

Inspired by spaceship and jet fighters, the manufacturers produced only 900 units of the model with 49 color options.

Similarly, other features of this Aventador include tri-dimensional air outlets, standard Nero aluminum wheels, a fully automatic projector beam, a carbon-fiber wing, a rear camera, etc.

Moreover, Lamborghini sold each 8,000 of this best and high-lap performance model for a staggering $573,966.

12. Lamborghini Urus

Price: $585,349

Lamborghini’s mos remarkable SUV ever produced Urus is the twelfth most expensive Lamborghini model of all time.

This SUV features a 4.0 L twin-turbocharged V8 engine with a top speed of 190 mph, making it the fastest SUV in the world.

Similarly, Urus features wild fighter jet-style Start-Stop buttons, ten piston brakes, 3 TFT displays, 4-5 seat layout, a center console, large space, etc.

Moreover, this genuine Lamborghini vibing SUV weighs only 4,900 lbs.

In addition, it offers seven driving modes (Strata, Sport, Corsa, Sabbia, Terra, Neve, Ego) more than any Lamborghini model.

Further, this SUV super sports car can give any road driving experiences (terrain, ice, sand, gravel, rocks) in a whopping $585,349 price tag.

11. Diablo GT 1

Price: $1 million

The coolest, rarest Lamborghini’s ever made, the 2000 Lamborghini Diablo GT 1 is the eleventh most expensive Lamborghini model of all time.

Similarly, this sports car features an aerospace technology system, carbon fibers all over its body except for doors and roofs, and three-piece OG wheels.

In addition, this model comprises rear carbon-fiber wings and airboxes.

Moreover, a 6.0L V-12 engine gives it a top speed of 210 mph.

Lamborghini produced only 80 of these models with a price tag of $1 million.

10. Mansory Carbonado Apertos

Price: $1.6 million

Mansory Carbonado Apertos is the tenth most expensive Lamborghini model of all time.

This model is the modified form of the Aventador Roadster. It comprises yellow and black leather stitching inside with a Carbonado emblem and a stealth black appearance outside.

Moreover, the manufacturers used carbon fiber panels on all of its body, including the exhaust system.

Similarly, its twin-turbocharged 6.5 L V-12 engine gives it a top speed of 236 mph.

Further, this biggest Aventador model costs a whopping $1.6 million.

9. Mansory Carbonado GT

Price: $2 million

On the ninth most expensive Lamborghini model of all time, we have Mansory Carbonado GT.

This finest customized Avendator features the white and black painted full carbon body with aggressive aerodynamics.

Similarly, this model includes a new exhaust system, updated bumper, new carbon-fiber trim, and a carbon fiber rear wing.

Moreover, its 6.5 L V12 engine produces an insane 1600 hp with a top speed of 230 mph.

This stunning model costs a whopping $2 million.

8. Pregunta Concept

Price: $2.1 Million

At first, this model appeared at the 1998 Paris Motor Show.

Similarly, it took a reference from the Diablo as a rear-wheel-drive having the same V12 engine with 530 hp accelerating at the top speed of 206 mph.

This futuristic edition of Lamborghini features jet fighter screens, fiber optic lights, a GPS, an updated air intake system, rear-view cameras, excluding mirrors.

Further, this matte grey-painted sports car costs a whopping $2.1 million.

7. Reventon Roadster

Price: $2.2 million

At first, this model appeared at a 2009 Frankfurt Motor Show.

Similarly, it features a 6.5 L V12 engine pumping 650 hp and accelerating with a maximum speed of 330 km/h.

Moreover, it comprises 3 TFT liquid displays, aggressive wedge shapes, an updated airflow system, safety roll bars, etc.

This four-wheel-drive roadster costs a whopping $2.2 million.

6. Aventador J

Price: $2.8 million

At first, this model appeared at the 2012 Geneva Motor Show.

Similarly, this sports car features a naturally aspirated 6.5 L V12 engine, a 7-speed automatic gearbox, carbon-fiber wings, and roll bars.

Moreover, this roofless and windowless model produces 700 hp giving a top speed of 200 mph.

Further, this roadster sold immediately after its launch at a staggering $2.8 million.

5. Sesto Elemento Concept

Price: $2.8 Million

Sesto Elemento Concept “Sixth Element” is the fifth most expensive Lamborghini model of all time.

At first, this sports car made its debut at the 2010 Paris Motor Show.

Similarly, it comprises E gear 6-speed ISR, aluminum and carbon frames, and an impressive aerodynamic system.

Moreover, a 5.2 L V-10 engine powers this model with a 570 hp and weighs 999 kg only.

Further, this concept car costs a staggering $2.8 million.

4. Miura Concept

Price: $3 million

On the fourth spot of the list, we have the mashup of classic and vintage Lamborghini, the Miura Concept.

At first, this concept car made its debut at Paley Center for Media Production in 2006.

Similarly, it features a 6.5 L  naturally aspirated V12 engine producing 640 hp. The car weighs under 1,500 kgs and boasts a 6-speed e-gear automatic transmission.

Moreover, Lamborghini Museum displays this rare concept car in Sant’ Agata Bolognese, Italy.

3. Egoista Concept

Price: $3 million

On the third spot comes another tribute car for Lamborghini’s 50th anniversary.

This jet-style concept car features a removable canopy door, one-seat cockpit, anti-radar body, and superb aerodynamics.

Moreover, a 5.2 L V-10 engine powers this sports car with 600 brake horsepower.

Further, Lamborghini Museum displays this single-seater advanced concept car in Sant’ Agata Bolognese, Italy.

2. Veneno

Price: $5.3 million

This model comes with a 6.5 L V12 engine producing 740 hp and a top speed of 221 mph.

Moreover, this sports car features improved aerodynamics, a front slopping bonnet, and long angular headlights.

In addition, Lamborghini produced only 4 Venenos with a spaceship design and carbon fiber everywhere, including in its rear wing.

Further, this limited production costs a staggering $5.3 million.

1. Veneno Roadster

Price: $8.3 million

Holding the top spot of the most expensive Lamborghini model is Veneno Roadster.

This roofless roadster comes with a 6.5 L V12 engine producing 750 hp and a top speed of 221 mph.

Moreover, it features a four-wheel driving system, seven-speed automated manual transmissions, active aerodynamics, and two doors.

Further, Lamborghini manufactured only 9 Veneno Roadsters at the most exclusive price of a staggering $8.3 million.
