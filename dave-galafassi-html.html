Dave Galafassi - Trivia, Family, Bio

Best known as a drummer for the Australian band, Gelbison. The band released two full-length albums. He is also the estranged husband of Toni Collette. The two announced their pending divorce in December of 2022, after a long trial separation. 

Before Fame

Gelbison was formed in 2000. 

Trivia

He and Collette were in a band together, Toni Collette & The Finish. After their album releases, the band embarked on a tour before eventually disbanding. 

Family Life

He was born in New South Wales, Australia. He and Collette married in 2003 and share two children, Sage and Arlo. 

Associated With

Duncan Phillips is a drummer who was also born in Australia.
