Stephanie Seymour Shows Off Her Toned Bikini Bod

Stephanie Seymour still looks amazing in a two piece.

The 45-year-old former Sports Illustrated model showed off her toned bod in a red, seashell print bikini recently during a vacation in St. Barts.

Seymour hit the waves with her husband Peter Brant and their children on their annual family holiday vacation.

The couple has been traveling to St. Barts for several years with their entire family, spending Christmas and New Year's Day in the tropical destination.
