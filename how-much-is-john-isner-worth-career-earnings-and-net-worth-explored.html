How much is John Isner worth? Career earnings and net worth explored

The Association of Tennis Professionals has placed American professional tennis player John Isner as high as world No. 8 in singles and No. 19 in doubles. He has also reached the US Open quarterfinals twice, in 2011 and 2018, the latter of which helped qualify him for an ATP Finals berth later that year.

How much does John Isner make a year?

According to some reports, circulating on the internet, John Isner makes $673,333.33 per year, $56,111.11 per month, and $12,948.72 per week.

Isner’s finest Grand Slam performance came at the 2018 Wimbledon Championships, as he reached his first major semifinals.

John Isner has also played in the longest and second-longest tennis matches in history; the 2010 Wimbledon first-round encounter against Nicolas Mahut and the 2018 Wimbledon semifinal against Kevin Anderson.

How much is John Isner worth?

Isner’s total career prize money is $19,336,515. His net worth is believed to be $14 million.

Isner and Jack Sock won the doubles title at Indian Wells in 2022, defeating Santiago González and Édouard Roger-Vasselin in the final.
