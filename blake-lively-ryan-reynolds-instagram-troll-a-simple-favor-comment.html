Blake Lively Trolled by Ryan Reynolds Yet Again Over Racy 'A Simple Favor' Instagram Promo

Happily married couple Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds have a long and endearing history of trolling one another on social media. And judging by the Deadpool star's most recent comment on one of his wife's Instagram posts, the jokes aren't about to end anytime soon.

Related Story

Lively decided to promote her new movie, A Simple Favor, this weekend by sharing a rather provocative photo on Instagram. In the shot, her character is fully dressed, but the man she's posing with is fully naked, and in a somewhat compromising position on a kitchen counter. Along with the caption, "My turn," as Lively takes control, it's completely impossible to look away:

Reynolds wasn't about to let a perfect opportunity like this one pass him by, and as always, he was ready with a prime response. Commenting on the photo of his wife getting up-close-and-personal with another man, Reynolds simply wrote, "He seems nice."

Lively and Reynolds recently celebrated their sixth wedding anniversary, and it's clear that their relationship is alive and well. Speaking to Good Morning America earlier in September, Lively revealed that they had a quiet anniversary celebration saying, "I worked a lot! Honestly, the best vacation we can have is to be able to do nothing with our family, so that's what we did."

As they have two young daughters, James and Inez, it's understandable that Reynolds and Lively like to relax at home in their time off.

But when it comes to their online trolling, it's pretty clear that neither Reynolds nor Lively will be giving up anytime soon.
