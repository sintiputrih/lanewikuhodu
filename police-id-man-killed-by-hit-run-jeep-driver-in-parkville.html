Police ID Man Killed By Hit-Run Jeep Driver In Parkville

James McCoy was found by first responders shortly after 9 p.m. on Wednesday, Jan. 24, after he was killed by a driver in a Jeep Liberty who sped away from the intersection of Loch Raven Boulevard and Sayward Avenue, according to officials on Friday.

According to a spokesperson from the Baltimore County Police Department, arriving officers located McCoy in the roadway, but the Jeep had fled the area. Now they are trying to locate the suspect and vehicle, which likely sustained damage in the fatal crash.

Anyone with information about the driver or vehicle has been asked to contact detectives at the department by calling (410) 307-2020.
