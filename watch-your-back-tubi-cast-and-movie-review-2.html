Meet The Cast Of Watch Your Back On Tubi

Watch Your Back Tubi cast consists of Jamal Gravy Woolard and Ametria Peridot in lead roles. Crystal The Doll and Makeiva Albritten also appear in the movie.

Jamal Gravy Woolard

Woolard appears in the main cast of Tubi’s Watch Your Back. He is a well-known American actor and rapper.

He came to public notice following his role as rapper The Notorious B.I.G. in George Tillman’s 2009 biographical film Notorious, based on the life of hip-hop artist The Notorious B.I.G.

The actor is also notable for portraying Biggie in the 2017 Tupac Shakur biopic All Eyez on Me, directed by Benny Boom.

Jamal is from Bensonhurst and Lafayette Gardens, Brooklyn. He raps under the name “Gravy” in real life. He is featured on Tupac Shakur’s Untouchable on Pac’s Life album.

In 2016, he starred as Marquese in Barbershop: The Next Cut.

Other notable credits include Dice City, The Return, Karma, The Bag Girls, Circumstances 3, Dirty Game, and Forbidden Daughter.

Ametria Peridot

Peridot plays the lead role of Kanisha in the newest Tubi crime drama. Watch Your Back marks the actress’ first lead role.

She is an actress, model singer, hip-hop artist, event host, philanthropist, and world traveler from Detroit. She has appeared in magazines all over the US, Canada, London, Nigeria, and Paris.

The actress has earned credits with various shows and productions. Ametria has been featured in multiple music videos for artists such as Philthy Rich and Sada Baby.

According to her IMDb profile, she made her acting debut playing the role of Irna Hatchett in the 2020 short drama Chantal on Chantal.

The following year, she appeared as Harmony on Kamal Smith’s He Played Me alongside Aqeel Ash-Shakoor, Devin Banks, Mica Bivings, Phillip Granger, Kaamel Hasuan (Savage Revenge), and Shelby Leigh.

She is active on Instagram under the username @ametria_peridot, with over 173k followers.

Crystal The Doll

Crystal the Doll, also known as Crystal Hughes, is a 32-year-old actress, lingerie model, celebrity Instagram star, and rapper.

People may remember Hughes as an artist who dropped the original tune “Bedroom.” She has appeared in several music videos, such as Brielle Lesley: Karma and Bedroom.

Moreover, the actress has been featured in multiple magazines, including BlackMen Magazine in 2015 and Straight Stuntin in 2016.

She started her acting journey by playing the role of Crystal in Brandon Cornett’s comedy film Child Support in 2019. The same year, she appeared in Buffed Up! The Movie, directed by Derek Scott.

Hughes has also contributed to several Detroit Hood Movies, such as Detroit Dreams, directed and written by Curtis Franklin aka Al Nuke.

Her Instagram account, @crystal.the.doll, has garnered over 432k followers. Her IG bio reads, “Yea I’m the girl from the Tubi Movies.”

Glen Tattoo Man Paige

Glen is an actor, comedian, voice-over artist, writer, and celebrity tattoo artist from Detroit. His creative tattoo works are available on his IG under the username @tattoomanpaigeink.

He is well-known for portraying Kyron in the 2017 drama Fireworks Fireworks starring, Aqeel Ash-Shakoor, La Shayla Hood, and Rodrina Jordan. The movie is written and co-directed by Kamal Smith.

In 2019, he appeared as Tattoo Man Paige in Child Support. In 2021, he landed the role of Pastor No Good in the comedy television series The Spot and appeared in two episodes.

His other acting credits include playing Vonn in Strange Fruit: Tale of a BlackGirl Lots, Mr. Tula in Asbury Park, Greedy in Chedda Boys, and Vante in Ultimate Betrayal.

Watch Your Back Movie Review

Lisa Brown’s Watch Your Back has received generally positive reviews from viewers. It is currently streaming on Tubi.

The movie revolves around Kanisha (Peridot) who is one of Detroit’s top strippers. A Beezy serves as the executive producer of this highly anticipated crime drama movie.

The film had its red carpet premiere on Friday, March 17, 2023, in Detroit, Michigan.

Watch Your Back dropped on Tubi just two days ago and fans couldn’t be happier. Viewers have thus taken their respective social media accounts to share their views and thoughts about the movie.

A Twitter user wrote, “Watch your back on Tubi so good 10/10.” Another added, “It was real good with the expected Tubi flaws.”

The movie as well acquired exclamations of satisfaction, awe, good, and intrigue from viewers. It features a small but contained cast each depicting significant roles.

It offers an adequate amount of twists and drama and fans are already hoping for part two to arrive soon. “They need to have Part 2 in the making already, ” tweeted one superfan.

Is There A Watch Your Back Part 2?

No, there is no Watch Your Back Part 2 on Tubi. However, fans have been relentless in their pursue for a second movie in the series.

Watch Your Back tells the story of one of Detroit’s top strippers Kanisha and the people involved with her.

The beautiful stripper specializes in effortlessly seducing and manipulating men for their money to fund her extravagant lifestyle. She is used to living a luxurious life out of their fortune.

Kanisha’s this practice eventually invites crime, drama, suspense, mystery, and many more into her and other’s life.

The movie offers an equal amount of emotion, heartbreak, friendship, fun, party, violence, romance, and a look at the stripper’s extravagant lifestyle, and their ways to achieve such goals.
