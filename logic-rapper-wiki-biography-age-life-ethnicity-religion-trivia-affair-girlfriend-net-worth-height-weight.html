Born on January 22, 1990, Logic (rapper) is an American rapper, musician, songwriter and record producer who, with the release of the mixtape Logic: The Mixtape in 2009, began his music career and has grown to fame with his fourth mixtape: Young Sinatra: Welcome to Forever.

He is also an artist who has released 5 studio albums as of 2019 and has written a novel named “Supermarket” in the New York Times that became a Best Seller.
