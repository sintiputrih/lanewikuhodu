Rapper 2Rare Age: How Old Is He?

Rapper 2rare, real name Naseem Young, is 22 years old in 2022.

2rare was born in the year 2000 and celebrates his birthday on August 12 every year. He was born in the city of Robert Lee Love, Philadelphia, PA, to a small family.

Even though his exact height is not known, he looks tall enough for an American man in his 20s.

2rare’s parents are originally from Ghana and they moved to the United States to provide a better upbringing and opportunities to their children. His was raised by his mother and grandmother, who had many stresses in life while he was growing up.

While growing up on the streets of North Philly, Naseem was exposed to the round side of the city in a very close and personal way. He witnessed cases of robberies and shootings but managed to avoid the perils in the city but find other ways to keep himself busy and enjoy his childhood.

He first delved into dancing, for which he had an incredible talent. He uploaded many videos to social media to express his love for dancing while also showing his talent to the real world.

One of his early videos is of him dancing to the energetic beats of Wu-Tang, which is a dance that originated in his hometown. As he was trying new things as a teenager, he began gravitating toward football. He dedicated much of his free time to football and eventually became good at it.

He was eventually named in the top 10 wide receivers in all of Philly at Lincoln High School.

What is 2rare’s Real Name & Why Is He Called 2rare?

Rapper 2rare’s real name is Naseem Young.

2rare was born and raised in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. His parents, however, are originally from Ghana.

While growing up in Philly while football in his mind, it was during one of the off-seasons that he rented a studio for a day and made music for the first time.

He recorded his first track in the studio, titled Switch Sides. It was an emotional track that expresses the feeling of being betrayed by close ones.

Now that the rap bug had caught hold of Naseem, his passion for football began to fall back. Once he decided that he was going to make it as a rapper, he decided to take his moniker of “2rare”, a combination of his favorite number and how he perceived himself to be different from everyone around him.

After that, he began dropping his real tracks that became singles like No Brotherly Love and Big Bad. What propelled 2rare very far and very quickly in the rap scene is his ability to connect with his audience and his knack for catchy hooks.

He signed his first deal with a joint venture with 10k Projects and Homemade Projects, and Internet Money Records even before he was legally allowed to drink in the United States.

He purposefully distanced himself from negative themes in his music in an attempt to take his sound to the next level. He wanted to create music that made people happy and want to dance.

After releasing several other party anthems, his biggest hit to date is Q-pid remix, where he got Lil Druk to collaborate. In the remix, he gives shoutouts to many of his favorite athletes while making this 1996 song a perfect fit for 2022.
