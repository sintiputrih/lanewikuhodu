Jennifer Lopez displays flat stomach in bikini

Jennifer Lopez may be a mother of two, but you'd never guess by looking at her abs. The 44-year-old flaunted her impressively toned stomach muscles via Twitter on Monday as she shared a snap taken by actress friend Leah Remini. JLo made sure all attention was on her midriff as she covered her face in large sunglasses and a straw trilby hat.
