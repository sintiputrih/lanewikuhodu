About

YouTuber and comedienne who won two 2013 Streamy Awards for her DailyGrace web series. After leaving MyDamnChannel, which owned DailyGrace, she created her own channel called It'sGrace, which has earned over 3 million subscribers. In April 2015, she began hosting a weekly primetime E! talk show called The Grace Helbig Show.

Before Fame

She uploaded her first YouTube web series, Bedtime Stories, in 2008. 

Trivia

She was given her first television correspondent position on the G4 network series Attack of the Show! She was also featured in the 2012 YouTube documentary Please Subscribe along with fellow YouTubers WheezyWaiter and MysteryGuitarMan. She released the book Grace's Guide: The Art of Pretending to be a Grown-Up in October 2014.

Family Life

She has an older brother named John and a younger brother named Tim, and is very close to her mother Theresa, who has appeared in several videos. She has been in a relationship with comedian Brooks Wheelan. She previously dated fellow web star Chester See.

Associated With

She made an appearance on the Late Show with David Letterman in December 2010.
