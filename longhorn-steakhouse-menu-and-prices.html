A Brief History of LongHorn Steakhouse

 News: LongHorn Steakhouse, initially named LongHorn Steaks Restaurant & Saloon, commenced its establishment in Atlanta in 1981. George McKerrow, Jr. and his close friend Brian founded the restaurant, and it swiftly became a favorite during the 1980s. In 1996, the establishment rebranded to LongHorn Steakhouse. Presently, there are around 559 LongHorn Steakhouse outlets, encompassing international branches. Darden Restaurants currently manages the operations of the restaurant.
