Biography

Steve Young was born in Salt Lake City, UT on October 11, 1961. Legendary San Francisco 49ers quarterback who followed Joe Montana and was named Super Bowl XXIX MVP. He became a popular ESPN football analyst after retirement. He starred in several commercials with Jerry Rice while they were teammates on the 49ers. After playing college football at BYU, he signed an unprecedented 10-year, $40 million contract with the Los Angeles Express of the USFL in 1984.
