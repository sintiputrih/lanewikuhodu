Ric Flair Gears Up for Public Appearances to Boost Exciting New Energy Drink

Wrestling legend and two-time WWE Hall of Famer Ric Flair is set to make appearances at Giant Eagle and Market District supermarkets in Ohio on September 11 and September 12. Flair will be introducing Wooooo! Energy, the exclusive energy drink of the Cleveland Cavaliers.

“Wooooo!” is a universally recognized catchphrase that represents success, celebration, and triumph. Flair’s iconic phrase has transcended the wrestling ring and become a symbol of victory. Fans will have the opportunity to meet ‘The Nature Boy’ and experience the power of “Wooooo!” in a can at these supermarket events.

To secure a place in line for the meet-and-greet with Ric Flair, attendees are required to register in advance for free on Eventbrite. Additionally, they must purchase a 6-pack of Wooooo Energy at the designated Giant Eagle or Market District locations. Thirty lucky fans will receive autographed 6-packs as part of a random giveaway.

The scheduled appearances are as follows:

Monday, September 11 in Columbus:

  • Pickerington Giant Eagle at 12:00pm (873 Refugee Road, Pickerington, OH 43147)
  • Gahanna Giant Eagle at 3:00pm (1250 North Hamilton Road, Columbus, OH 43230)
  • Westerville Market District at 6:00pm (650 North State Street, Westerville, OH 43082)

Tuesday, September 12 in Cleveland:

  • Water Tower Giant Eagle at 12:00pm (27264 Lorain Road, North Olmsted, OH 44070)
  • Painesville Giant Eagle at 3:00pm (1201 Mentor Avenue, Painesville, OH 44077)
  • Stow Hudson Giant Eagle at 6:00pm (1700 Norton Road, Stow, OH 44224)

In addition to these Ohio appearances, Ric Flair will be making an appearance at Giant Eagle Supermarkets in the Pittsburgh area on September 13. Fans can register on Eventbrite, purchase a 6-pack of “Wooooo! Energy,” and have the chance to receive an autographed 6-pack.

The scheduled appearances in Pittsburgh are as follows:

Wednesday, September 13 in Pittsburgh:

  • Shaler Giant Eagle at 11 AM (1671 Butler Plank Road, Glenshaw, PA 15116)
  • Monroeville Giant Eagle at 2 PM (4010 Monroeville Boulevard, Monroeville, PA 15146)
  • Century Plaza Giant Eagle at 5 PM (9901 Mountain View Drive, West Mifflin, PA 15122)

This partnership between Ric Flair’s “Wooooo! Energy” and Giant Eagle signifies the merging of two iconic brands that represent triumph, legacy, and boundless passion. The “Wooooo!” chant, born in the wrestling ring, has become a universal anthem of celebration and victory, and it now fuels the spirit of champions and fans alike.

Don’t miss the chance to meet the legendary Ric Flair and raise a can of “Wooooo! Energy” in celebration of success and achievement.
