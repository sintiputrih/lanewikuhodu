Jake Epstein Net Worth

$300 thousand

Jake Epstein is a Canadian actor. He first debuted in 1999 with a role in the TV series Ricky’s Room. In the 2000s, he initially appeared in the TV movie Mom’s Got a Date with a Vampire, as well as in the Disney Channel Original Movie Quints. His first starring role on TV was as Cam Dunleavy in the sci-fi series The Zack Files. From 2002 to 2009, he gained wider recognition for his role as Craig Manning in the Canadian teen series Degrassi: The Next Generation. During this period, he also started his film career with 2007’s Charlie Bartlett. In the 2010s, he became further known for his role as Chuck Russink in Designated Survivor. As of 2017, he also plays Brian Altman in Suits.
