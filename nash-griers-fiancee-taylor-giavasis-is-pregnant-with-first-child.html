Nash Griers Fiancee Taylor Giavasis Is Pregnant With First Child

Make way for another social media savant! Vine star Nash Grier and his fiancée, Taylor Giavasis, announced on Friday, April 19, that they are expecting their first child.

“1 + 1 = 3,” Grier, 21, tweeted with a photo of himself cradling Giavasis’ baby bump in a field of flowers.

He added: “I was holding Taylor’s belly the whole time after I proposed idk how y’all missed that.” The YouTuber shared more pics from the gorgeous maternity shoot via his Instagram Story.

Giavasis, meanwhile, tweeted a solo shot showing off her growing belly in lingerie. “My angel,” the glowing mom-to-be captioned the post.

“As long as I can remember I’ve wanted to be a mommy,” the 21-year-old wrote via Instagram. “I’ve always been enthralled with pregnancy and the joy/strength that come with motherhood. I am honored to be able to experience such a gift.”

Grier previously shot down pregnancy rumors in December 2018. “Thanks for the wishes but we’re not pregnant,” he tweeted at the time. “We’ve just been eating good @taylorgiavasis.”

Upon revealing the happy news on Friday, the internet star poked fun at the message. “Little did we know,” he wrote.
