Get To Know MIC Lily Ludovici Gray! Her Age, Family, Boyfriend

Made in Chelsea: Bali is officially here and this MIC spin-off features OG stars like Sam Prince and Inga Valentiner as well as newcomers like Lily Ludovici Gray and Ruby’s love interest, Renan, all the way from London to the global destination in Indonesia. Keep reading this wiki article to learn everything about Lily Gray.

Lily Ludovici Gray On Made In Chelsea (MIC)

Made In Chelsea: Bali ran for four days straight from December 20th to 23rd, giving viewers an explosion of drama, from Yasmine and Issy’s heated argument at the dinner table over Miles Nazaire and Harvey to Ruby and Melissa’s confrontation with Sam Prince and his girlfriend Inga.

Since there were a lot of OG’s as well as new stars in the newest spinoff of Made In Chelsea, here’s a list of all the cast members: Ollie Locke, Gareth Locke, Emily Blackwell, Melissa Tattam, Sam Prince, Inga Valentiner, Issy Francis-Baum, Yasmine Zweegers, Miles Nazaire, Julius Cowdrey, Lily Gray, Harvey Armstrong, Charlie Wicks, Arabella Ovendon, and Renan.

Besides these names, there were also some unfamiliar faces in the series, like a girl who claimed Sam had liked some of her pictures on Instagram and a man called Rene, who Ollie claimed went on a date with Ruby.

Lily Ludovici Gray joined Made In Chelsea for its 24th season shortly before the Bali spin-off aired. She was Tiffany Watson’s bridesmaid. Lily is based across both London and Bali.

In case you felt like you had seen Lily before in MIC right, you were not mistaken. The social media manager briefly appeared in Season 10 of the show, when she threatened to come between Tiffany’s sister, Lucy Watson, and her boyfriend, James Dunmore.

Furthermore, Lily is besties with Love Island 2022 winner Ekin-Su. MIC star has plenty of pictures on her Instagram featuring Ekin-Su. Love Island star has even given an interview, where she talked about Lily joining Made In Chelsea.

Ekin-Su shared that Lily is very Made in Chelsea because she’s “posh.” She added that Lily would be great on the show and that she would be supporting her mate wholeheartedly.

Before Lily stepped on MIC, Ekin-Su also shared some words of wisdom with her best mate on how to be confident. Love Island 2022 winner said, “I’m like ‘Lily just be you. Don’t be someone else, don’t be scared if a girl is going to attack you. I’m fearless, be fearless.'”

Is Lily Ludovici Gray On Instagram?

Lily is super popular on Instagram as she has close to 44 thousand followers on her @lilyludovici IG account. Lily’s IG is all about her spectacular holiday pictures. She has also featured her old friend Tiffany Watson and other MIC stars like Inga Valentiner and Emily Blackwell on her Instagram.

Besides Instagram, Lily uses Facebook and Twitter.

Lily Ludovici Gray Age

Made In Chelsea star Lily Ludovici Gray’s age was 29 as of December 2022. Her birthday falls on May 25th. As Lily shared on Instagram, Lily celebrated her 27th  birthday in her room with her friend Pablo Fernandez as the whole world was shut down due to COVID.

Lily Ludovici Gray Job

Coming to Lily Ludovici Gray’s job, the reality TV star runs the social media for Hailey Bieber’s skincare brand, Rhodes Skincare, which is an award-winning, cruelty-free, natural, and plant-based beauty brand.

In addition to being a social media manager, Lily is a photographer as well. She shares her photography on @lilyludoviciphotography IG handle. In the Instagram highlights section, she has shared photographs of celebs such as Lottie Moss and Tiffany Watson taken by her, which all have ended up in The Daily Mail. Also, she has taken portraits of Emily Blackwell, Harry Baron, and Olivia Bentley.

As an Instagram influencer, she promotes a brand called Bali Body.

Lastly, Lily is a makeup artist well. She did makeup work for Model Mayhem, based in London, UK. According to Model Mayhem, she only worked there from January 17, 2012, to February 15, 2012.

Lily Ludovici Gray Family

Talking about Lily’s family, her parents are Ashley James Rowland Gray and Annabel Ludovici.

Made in Chelsea star’s dad Ashley Gray was born on May 12, 1964. His age was 58 as of December 2022. As per thepeerage.com, he completed his education at Grenville College, Devon, England. Ashley majorly earned his living as a media relations consultant.

Lily’s dad married Lily’s mom, Annabel Ludovici, in 1990. Annabel studied at UAL: University of the Arts London, Chelsea College of Arts, St. Martin’s School of Art, UK, and Downe House School.

Lily’s parents gave birth to two other kids besides Lily. They are Theodore and Louis Gray.

According to Theodore’s Facebook, he is a producer/director/art dealer by profession. He studied Communications at Richmond, The American International University in London. On the other hand, Louis is a musician/songwriter.
