Tori Spellings Finances Continue To Suffer As Her Estranged Husband Moves In With New Girlfriend

Highlights

  • Dean McDermott is reportedly moving in with his new girlfriend Lily Calo while his divorce from Tori Spelling is still pending.
  • Tori Spelling seems to be okay with Dean moving on and has even been linked to someone new herself.
  • Tori has been living in an RV with their kids due to their home being treated for mold, and they have a history of financial problems that may be affecting the divorce proceedings.

Dean McDermott and Tori Spelling’s divorce not be official yet, but he’s reportedly already moving in with his new girlfriend Lily Calo, despite his estranged wife’s alleged financial problems.

"Dean’s saying he and Lily are in love and ready for the next step," a source explained, OK! Magazine reports. "She’s been amazingly supportive of his journey to sobriety, and they’re spending so much time together, it just made sense for him to move in.”

Tori Is Reportedly Okay With Dean Moving On

While Dean’s decision to move in with Lily may seem sudden to fans, given that he only announced his divorce from Tori in June, the insider says Spelling has no hard feelings towards him. She also appeared to be moving on, having been romantically linked to Ryan Cramer last November.

"It was like, whatever makes you happy. Tori has moved on too," the source added.

Tori Has Been Living In An RV With Their Kids

In August, it was reported that Tori was living in an RV with her six kids following her split from Dean and while their home was being treated for a mold infection.

Posting to social media in July, Tori explained how their infested home led to multiple hospital visits before they moved into the RV after being between hotels for a bit.

“Here we are again at Urgent Care. We’ve all been on this continual spiral of sickness for months. Sick. Get better. To get sick again,” she told her followers.

“Used to think… well that’s what happens when you have young kids in school. They just continually bring sicknesses home,” Tori continued. “But, when it gets to the point where they are at home sick more than being in school we had to reassess what was going on.”

Tori and Dean have a long history of financial problems, so it’s possible that their money situation is slowing down their divorce proceeding, as there hasn’t been confirmation of an official divorce, though they both clearly seem to be moving on.
