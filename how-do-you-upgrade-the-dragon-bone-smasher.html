What does the Dragon Bone Smasher scale with?

The Dragon Bone Smasher has a B Strength scaling as well. Used with two hands and fully upgraded, you'll get an attack rating north of 400, especially if you have a high enough strength stat. Even better, you can still enchant the Dragon Bone Smasher even further to make it deal even more damage.

Is the Dragon Bone Smasher a good weapon?

Demon's Souls' Dragon Bone Smasher is one of the best Strength weapons in the game, but to get it players will need to understand World Tendency. Getting a powerful weapon can make all the difference in Demon's Souls, and few are as powerful as the Dragon Bone Smasher.

Can you upgrade the Dragon Bone Crusher?

Dragon Bone Smasher can be upgraded up to +5 at Blacksmith Ed using Colorless Demon Souls.

Can Dragon Bone Smasher be parried?

Players will need to have a weapon that allows them to parry in order to perform the maneuver, and they'll only be able to parry against certain kinds of attacks. Acid, Poison, Death Cloud spells, and the Dragon God's fire breath are not attacks a player can parry.

    Does killing black phantoms increase World Tendency?

    Killing "named" NPC Black Phantoms increases your World Tendency towards White.

    How many people have died from pure black tendency?

    The primary way of lowering World Tendency is to die in Body Form. If a player dies while in this form, their World Tendency shifts towards black by -30%, meaning it only takes four deaths to go Pure Black from neutral.

    How do I get rid of black tendency?

    If you are in Human Form but want to avoid losing Tendency, go to the Nexus and jump off the high staircase to kill yourself and return to Phantom Form. Similarly, if you need to get any World to Pure Black Tendency repeatedly go into Human Form using Stone of Ephemeral Eyes, then kill yourself in that World.

    Is scraping spear good?

    The Scraping Spear's special ability is that it reduces the durability of enemy equipment with every successful attack. Landing enough attacks can cause the enemy's equipment to break, reducing their damage or defense. This has no effect on monsters, but can be useful against invaders and other humanoids.

    Does killing Garl Vinland affect character tendency?
