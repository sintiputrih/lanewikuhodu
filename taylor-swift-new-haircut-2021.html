Fans love Taylor Swift's new 2021 haircut

Taylor Swift just debuted a brand new 2021 haircut in an interview for PEOPLE’s YouTube channel, and fans are totally obsessed with it.

Throughout her 15 years in the music industry, Taylor Swift has had a lot of different hairstyles.

From short to long, fringe to no fringe and straight to curly, she’s pretty much had it all.

Now, she’s just debuted a brand new haircut for 2021, and here’s what it looks like…

Taylor Swift’s new haircut revealed

Taylor did an interview with PEOPLE for their YouTube channel this week, and fans instantly noticed that she’s got a new hairstyle.

She’s ditched the dark blonde fringed style that she’s sported for a while and gone for something a little different.

Well, it’s not THAT different. She still has her iconic fringe, but she’s dyed it a much warmer toned strawberry blonde with an ombre effect on the ends.

As well as the colour, the style has changed too. Taylor has left the hair straightener behind and gone for a more tousled, messy look.

Taylor hasn’t shown off the new hairstyle on her Instagram yet though, and the PEOPLE interview is the only evidence we have that she’s changed her hair.

Taylor has been ginger before

Although Taylor is best known for her sleek, blonde locks, this isn’t the first time she’s dabbled with a bit more colour.

In fact, Taylor has often added a little ginger tone to her hair before, especially in the early days of her career.

She even sported red hair in 2018 when she appeared in Sugarland’s Babe music video and fans were totally obsessed!
