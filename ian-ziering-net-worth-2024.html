Early Life and Career Beginnings

Ian Ziering was born in Newark, New Jersey, and raised in West Orange. He began his acting career at the age of 12, performing in commercials before landing roles in soap operas and television series. His breakthrough came in 1990 when he was cast as Steve Sanders in the hit series “Beverly Hills, 90210.”

“Beverly Hills, 90210” and Rise to Fame

Ziering’s role in “Beverly Hills, 90210” catapulted him to fame. The show was a massive success, running for ten seasons and earning Ziering a significant portion of his wealth. His portrayal of Steve Sanders, a wealthy and charming high school student, made him a household name.

Post “Beverly Hills, 90210” Career

After “Beverly Hills, 90210” ended in 2000, Ziering continued to work in television and film. He appeared in various TV shows and movies, including “JAG,” “What I Like About You,” and “Domino.” He also ventured into voice acting, lending his voice to characters in animated series like “Biker Mice from Mars” and “Spider-Man: The Animated Series.”

“Dancing with the Stars” and Other Ventures

In 2007, Ziering competed in the fourth season of “Dancing with the Stars,” further increasing his visibility and earning potential. He also launched his own production company, IZZY Productions, and produced the hit Syfy film series “Sharknado,” which became a cult phenomenon and added to his wealth.
