Law &amp; Order: SVU to Tackle Johnny Depp-Amber Heard Trial

Law & Order: Special Victims Unit is about to take on a hot topic.

The long-running crime NBC series will be putting their own spin on the Amber Heard–Johnny Depp defamation trial that’s made headlines for months.

New set photos reveal that the cast is working on an upcoming episode in New York City, which seemingly recreates the Heard-Depp trial.

Click inside to read more…

Photos taken from the set see Detective Olivia Benson (Mariska Hargitay) leaving a courtroom alongside a female celebrity, played by Julia Goldani Telles.

Fans holding signs are at either side of them, either in support of Kelsey (presumably Julia’s character’s name), or Austin, presumably her ex, channeling the same chants of “Team Amber” or “Team Johnny” that the public latched onto over the summer as the trial went viral. See the photos at ETCanada.

Specifics about the storyline have yet to be revealed. Season 24 of Law & Order: SVU arrives later this fall.
