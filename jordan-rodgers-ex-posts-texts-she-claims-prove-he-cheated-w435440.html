Jordan Rodgers Ex Posts Texts She Claims Prove He Cheated

Going back through the text archives. Jordan Rodgers’ ex-girlfriend Brittany Farrar continued her Instagram rant by posting (and then deleting) text messages that she claims prove the Bachelorette star cheated on her during their relationship.

According to screenshots posted on Friday, August 19, Farrar posted a receipt for a bouquet of roses that she had mentioned in a previous post. She alleges Rodgers, 27, purchased the blooms for his mistress in June 2014. The receipt reads: “Happy Birthday To My Favorite Bella. Miss You Can’t Wait To See That Beautiful Smile Again. From, Your Favorite Football Player Wanna-Be Actor.”

“The Original Rose: The receipt for a giant bouquet of roses Jordan bought for the girl he cheated on me with in Louisiana. (The first of many I would later find out about),” Farrar captioned the Instagram post. “He met her while shooting Pitch Perfect 2 (he was deep in the background pretending to be one of the Green Bay Packers — the closest he’s ever been to making an NFL team). And for the devil’s advocate I’ve included screenshots of texts with dates to show we were a couple when he bought his favorite Bella some very special roses. And also one of the many, many texts he sent after we broke up.”

In a follow-up Instagram post, the personal trainer shared three alleged screenshots of text message exchanges she claims she had with the former football player. In the texts — which are dated June 26, 2014, the same time period that Rodgers allegedly purchased the bouquet of flowers — Farrar argues with Rodgers for not texting her the night before.

“Sooo no text from u last night when u got home.. How come? … Don’t think I would have appreciated a text? … Really sincere,” she allegedly wrote, to which Rodgers replied, “My bad … Like I said it was a long night.”

More than a month later, the aspiring sports broadcaster allegedly sent Farrar a series of texts, which she seemingly ignored, writing, “How do you not respond to any of that all day?? You really have no feelings for me anymore do you? I don’t get it britt. I can’t sleep I can’t eat, I’m losing a s–t load of weight I’m depressed. I’m beating myself up enough about this already. Please just talk to me.”

The next day, Rodgers reportedly sent another message: “Good morning love of my life. Just letting you know I’m not giving up.”
