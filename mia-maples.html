Career and Professional Life

Mia Maples created her YouTube account on February 15, 2013. The first video on her channel, which was originally named ‘Ivorygirl48,’ was a makeup tutorial titled ‘Neutral Smokey Eye ~ Great for Beginners’.

The second video featured her brother doing her makeup which was published in August 2013.

She came up with a Valentine’s Day special video on January 19, 2014. Moreover, in this video titled ‘DIY Decorations for Valentine’s Day & Ways to Spice up your room + A Gift Idea’, she demonstrated how to spruce up a room with do-it-yourself décor.

She then posted several videos on beauty and lifestyle, arts and crafts, food, etc. She used the lyrics of Shawn Mendes’ “Treat You Better” for a “Song Lyric Prank on My Friends Boyfriend” video.

Furthermore, on April 1, 2018, the young diva shared her shopping experience on the popular website ALIEXPRESS in the video ‘Trying on ALIEXPRESS Prom Dresses!! *Huge Success* & Giveaway’.

She also gave some online shopping tips to girls in the vlog. Five months later, Maple uploaded a video that would become the most viewed video on her channel.
