What time is Jeopardy! on and what channel is it on?

Jeopardy! is a syndicated program, with channels and times varying by area.

Viewers can go to jeopardy.com/watch to search when and where you can watch the show.

You can stream past episodes of Jeopardy! on Netflix or go to a live taping of the show in Los Angeles.

Who are the upcoming guest host?

As the game show continues its search for the next permanent host, viewers will see some fun celebrity guest host.

This year began with Ken Jennings, who set the record for the longest winning streak in Jeopardy! history after 74 consecutive wins.

Executive producer Mike Richards, Katie Couric, and Dr. Oz have all taken turns hosting the show the past few months.

Going forward the line-up includes Aaron Rogers from April 5- 16, Anderson Cooper from April 19- 30, Bill Whitaker from May 3- 14, and Mayim Bialik from May 31- June 11.

Savannah Guthrie and Dr. Sanjay Gupta will also be hosting but have not announced their dates.

How do I become a contestant?

To become a contestant on Jeopardy! you have to be at least 18 years old unless you're competing in the Teen Tournament which allows ages 13 to 17.

The first step to be eligible is to pass the 50-question online Jeopardy! or Anytime Test.

The Jeopardy! Test is an annual event, scheduled for a specific date and time.

TELEVISION

The Anytime Test has the same format, but as its name suggest, is available to be taken at anytime.

Once you pass the test, potential contestants are placed into a random selection process for an invitation to an audition.

Potential contestants who do well at the audition are then placed into the contestant pool and may be invited to compete up to 18 months from the audition date.
