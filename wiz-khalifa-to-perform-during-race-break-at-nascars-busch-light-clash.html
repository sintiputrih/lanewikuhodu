WizKhalifa to perform during race break at NASCARs Busch Light Clash

American rapper and songwriter Wiz Khalifa is set to perform during the race break at NASCAR’S Busch Light clash at the Coliseum.

NASCAR announced today that the multiple award-winning artiste will be performing at the Busch Light clash on the 5th of February, 2023.

The performance for the Busch Light Clash will air live on FOX as part of NASCAR’s season-opening celebration inside the iconic Los Angeles Memorial Coliseum.

“Wiz Khalifa is a gifted and talented performer with a worldwide following,” said Ben Kennedy, NASCAR’s senior vice president for racing development and strategy.

“I know the fans will be on their feet during his performance at the Busch Light Clash as we celebrate the beginning of our 75th Anniversary season together.”

Reacting to the announcement of his performance at the Busch Light Clash, Wiz Khalifa said; “The NASCAR cars will bring the noise, and I’ll bring the party”.

“We’re going to have a great time celebrating together at the Busch Light Clash at the Coliseum, and I can’t wait to see everybody there.”

Wiz Khalifa has won two GRAMMY Awards and a Golden Globe Award. He also won “Best New Artist” at the BET Awards and “Top New Artist” at the Billboard Music Awards.
