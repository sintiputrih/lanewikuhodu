Roberto Clemente's Top 5 Most Expensive Baseball Cards

The late Pittsburgh Pirates legend Roberto Clemente made his mark on Major League Baseball for both the way he used to play and what he did off the diamond as a humanitarian. Naturally, his baseball cards from the 1990s are highly sought-after collectibles and have sold for ridiculous amounts at auctions. Let's begin by jumping into the exciting hobby of sports card collecting with a countdown of Roberto Clemente baseball cards that lead to extraordinary sale prices.

 

The Chase for Rare Roberto Clemente Cards
