Here is Who Plays Cortana in the Halo TV Series

Let’s meet Cortana in the upcoming Halo TV series as the latest trailer of the show reveals the beloved AI for the first time!

For Halo fans, Master Chief might be the superior character, but Cortana always has a special place in every Halo player’s heart.

While the upcoming TV adaptation of the Halo franchise tries to tell its own story, it attempts to remain as much loyal as it can to the video game series. Hopefully, you can see this loyalty in the adaptation of Cortana.

Jen Taylor Is the Voice Actress Behind Cortana in Halo TV Series, Similar to the Original Game

Last night, Paramount Plus shared a brand new look to the Halo TV series, showing off more characters from the series, including Cortana and her creator, Dr. Catherine Halsey.

Jen Taylor, the legendary voice actress of Cortana in the Halo video game franchise, will be back for the role of her character in the TV series as well. Similar to the video games, you can see Cortana as a blueish AI, though her outfit and face seem different from the game.

Despite having Jen Taylor as Cortana, she will not reprise her role for Dr. Halsey in the TV series as the character will be played by Natascha McElhone.

In addition to Cortana, the new trailer of the TV series revealed more about the concept of the story and the enemies Master Chief is going to fight against during his unforgiving mission.

The first episode of the Halo TV series will officially go live on March 24 this year on Paramount Plus. Thus far, only one season has been confirmed for the Halo series, though it could be renewed for more if fans like it.
