Le’ Dor Milteer Bio – Wiki

Le’Dor Milteer is an American Executive Producer and Host of AIB Network’s “iN Contact” public affairs TV show. In 2019, she is starring in “In a Man’s World,” on Bravo which premiered October 1.

In 2017, she and Jody Reichel were vying for the City Council District 4 seat on the Sandy Springs City Council,in northern Fulton County, Georgia, the United States in the Nov. 7 nonpartisan election. Milteer, who is a journalist, said she wanted to bring new ideas to the city.

“I am running to bring a fresh point of view to Sandy Springs District 4 concerning traffic planning, public safety, walkability/livability, and responsible development,” she said of her top three campaign issues. She lost to Jody.
