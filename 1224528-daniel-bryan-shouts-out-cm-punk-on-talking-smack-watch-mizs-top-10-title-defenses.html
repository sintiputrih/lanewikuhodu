Daniel Bryan Shouts Out CM Punk On Talking Smack

WWE Superstar Daniel Bryan makes regular appearances on Talking Smack and this past weekend was no different. Bryan wants to return to his glory days from year’s ago, and he name-dropped CM Punk when talking about it.

Daniel Bryan said that he was no longer putting himself on the backburner and will instead be putting himself first before anyone else. Bryan said, “I want you to give this message to Roman Reigns. I am no longer putting myself on the back burner. I am no longer putting other people first. I am going out to take what’s mine and that’s the reign that I had before CM Punk and that’s the best damn wrestler in the world.”
