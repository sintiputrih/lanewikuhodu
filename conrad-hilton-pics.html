Conrad Hilton: Pics Hollywood Life

You may not be all that familiar with Conrad Hughes Hilton, but you probably already know all about his sister. His sister is none other than reality TV superstar/model/singer/businesswoman 39-year-old Paris Hilton. And he’s got some other siblings as well. He also grew up with brother 30-year-old Barron Hilton II and 36-year-old sister Nicky Hilton Rothschild. He, of course, is the youngest sibling at 26 years old. 

Conrad was born on March 3, 1994 in New York, NY, but him and his siblings were all raised by their parents Richard Hilton and Kathy Hilton in Beverly Hills, California. And in case you didn’t already know, yes, he has been living quite the lavish life with his family, as they have inherited quite a lot from his great-grandfather Conrad Hilton, aka the founder of Hilton Hotels, which is only one of the largest hotel brands in the world.
