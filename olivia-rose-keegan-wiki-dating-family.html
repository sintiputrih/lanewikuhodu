Know About Olivia Rose Keegan; Wiki, Dating, Family, TV Shows, Facts

Published Date: 18 Mar, 2020 Updated date: 18 Mar, 2020 By Celebs Podium

Olivia Rose Keegan has been active on the showbiz since an early age. Besides her acting talent, she is also a passionate singer, who has released numerous tracks.

Olivia Rose Keegan is an American actress who earned her recognition for her role as Claire Brady in the daytime soap opera Days of Our Lives. She appeared in a lead role as Brianna in the 2014 mystery film Amnesiac.

Olivia Rose Keegan Wiki, Family

Olivia was born on 22 November 1999 in San Rafael, California. She is the youngest daughter of Kevin Keegan and Julie Keegan. Her father is a custom home builder based in Los Angeles. He is the co-founder of the building and development firm, Ten Eyck and Keegan, Inc. She has an older sister named Jessica Keegan.

Keegan with a height of 5 feet 4 inches attended her high school in her hometown. She moved to Los Angeles to pursue an acting & singing career at the age of 12.

Career; Movies, TV Shows

Olivia made her movie debut from the 2012 short movie Maggie Clayton. Moreover, she was cast in a lead role as Lily in the movie Hand of God. In the same year, she appeared in a teen sitcom A.N.T. Farm. Her notable movie credits include Ashley (2013), Amnesiac (2014), and Salvation Street (2015).
