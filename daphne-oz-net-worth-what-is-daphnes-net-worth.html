Daphne Oz Net Worth: What Is Daphnes Net Worth?

Daphne Nur Oz has an estimated net worth of $12 Million. She accrued this worth through her profession as a television host, food writer and chef.

Oz was one of five co-hosts on the ABC daytime talk show The Chew for the show’s first six seasons and was a co-host of the syndicated talk/cooking show The Good Dish.

Her first book, a National Bestseller, The Dorm Room Diet: The 10-Step Program for Creating A Healthy Lifestyle Plan That Really Works (Newmarket Press, 2006) details the tips and tricks Oz used to successfully navigate the unique “Danger Zones” of college life to create a healthy lifestyle and sustainably lose 40lbs.

Oz is an ambassador for and helped to establish HealthCorps, a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit that equips teenagers with nutrition, exercise, and stress management education in over 50 schools nationwide. She also serves as a member of the board of Children’s Board at Columbia.

Daphne Oz has been married to John Jovanovic since August 2010 and together they share four children; Jovan Jovanovic, Philomena Bijou Jovanovic, Giovanna Ines Jovanovic and Domenica Celine Jovanovic.
