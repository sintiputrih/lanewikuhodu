Mark Cuban parents: Meet Norton Cuban, Shirley Cuban

Mark Cuban is a popular businessman. He is a billionaire entrepreneur, television personality, and media proprietor.

Mark Cuban was born on July 31, 1958, and according to Forbes and other websites, his net worth is an estimated $4.8 billion. Cuban is rated higher on the 2020 Forbes list of the richest people in the United States of America.

Both the Dallas Mavericks, a professional basketball franchise in the National Basketball Association (NBA), and 2929 Entertainment are under his ownership. He is also a co-owner of 2929 Entertainment.

Mark Cuban parents: Meet Norton Cuban, Shirley Cuban

Cuban is a native of Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. His father, Norton Cuban, was a car upholstery specialist. Shirley, according to Cuban, has “a different job or different career goal every other week.”

Shirley Cuban is the mother of Mark Cuban, the owner of the Dallas Mavericks. The Dallas Morning News reports that Cuban died on Friday at the suburban Pittsburgh home where she and her husband, Norton, reared sons Mark, Brian, and Jeff. His mother passed away following a long fight with lung cancer, according to Brian Cuban.
