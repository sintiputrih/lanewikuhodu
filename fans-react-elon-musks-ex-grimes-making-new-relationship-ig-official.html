Fans React To Elon Musk's Ex Grimes Making Her New Relationship IG Official

Highlights

  • Grimes and Musk shocked fans with unique baby names but have since split. Now, she's been seen with EDM artist Anyma, revealing a new romance.
  • Fans are thrilled Grimes moved on from her relationship with Musk. Anyma and Grimes have officially debuted their romance on Instagram.
  • Us Weekly reported Anyma and Grimes will perform together at Coachella. Grimes previously discussed her pregnancy and breakup with Musk.

Elon Musk and Grimes (whose real name is Claire Boucher) were first linked in 2018. The couple got a lot of attention when they announced the birth of their son in 2020. It was not just any other celebrity birth because they chose an incredibly unusual name for their kid, X Æ A-12. In 2022, they announced the birth of their second child, Exa Dark Sideræl Musk, who was born via surrogate.

Then, in September 2023, the couple secretly had a third child. They named him Techno Mechanicus. To say that they have had a complicated relationship would be an accurate description. However, Grimes and Musk have since officially ended their relationship, and she appears to have found love again. The musician debuted her romance on Instagram by posting a photo of herself kissing EDM artist Anyma.

What Have Fans Said About Grimes' New Relationship?

Fans have reacted with positivity to the post of Grimes and Anyma. "They gonna have a baby, and its name is going to be a literal twig from the forest," one fan joked. "Anyma and Grimes the EDM couple final boss," another wrote. And for those who don't know who he is, some fans have taken the time to educate others. "Why is everyone acting like we don't know him? This is the DJ of the moment, Matteo - aka @anyma. Grimes even has music with him … he has been getting a lot of popularity lately for his shows, the visuals, and maybe his good looks," they wrote.

The singer's fans are also happy she is no longer with Musk, and this is reflected in the comments. "MOTHER IS FREE FROM HER SHACKLES," one excited fan wrote. "Whoever that is, it's a f***ing relief to see," another commented. "Girlie, this is a HUGE step up from the wrinkled raisin from your past," a fan commented, referring to Musk.

It's unclear how long they have been a couple, but Grimes and Anyma have collaborated before this announcement. According to Us Weekly, Anyma appeared in Grimes' song, Welcome to the Opera, in June 2023. The publication also notes that both musicians will perform at the 2024 Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival in April.

What Has Grimes Said About Her Relationship With Elon Musk?

Grimes and Musk were an unexpected couple, which made them all the more interesting. In March 2020, the Shinigami Eyes musician discussed her relationship with Rolling Stone, informing fans of how her pregnancy changed her life.

"For a girl, it's sacrificing your body and your freedom. It's a pretty crazy sacrifice and only half of the population has to do it. It was really profound to me when I decided I was going to do it," she said. "... I'm just like, 'I have sacrificed my power in this moment. I have, like, capitulated.' And I have spent my whole life avoiding that situation. I have never capitulated to anything, so it was just a profound commitment."

Musk and Grimes ended their relationship several times. She announced another breakup in 2022 following the birth of their second child. "Me and E have broken up *again* since the writing of this article, haha, but he's my best friend and the love of my life, and my life and art are forever dedicated to The Mission now," she wrote on X (formerly Twitter) in March 2022. But there was a time when they were committed and in love, as she revealed in her Rolling Stone interview.

"I do actually just really love my boyfriend. So I was like, 'You know, sure,'" she said of agreeing to have a child.
