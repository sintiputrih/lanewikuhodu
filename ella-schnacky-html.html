Ella Schnacky - Age, Family, Bio

Social star who has accumulated over 1.1 million followers to her EllaSchnackyy TikTok account, where she shares a variety of pranks, skits, and other comedic videos. She is also known for being the younger sister of Noah Schnacky. 

Before Fame

At the age of four, she was cast in a stage production of The Wizard of Oz. 

Trivia

In 2010, she was a backup dancer on an episode of Extreme Makeover: Home Edition. 

Family Life

She has two brothers, Noah and Colby, as well as two sisters named Allie and Noelle. She also has a dog named Dash.  

Associated With

She lent her voice to the film Turtle Tale, which starred Mikey Bolts and Tom Arnold.
