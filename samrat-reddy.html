Biography/Wiki

Samrat Reddy is 37 years-old who was born on 6 April 1983 in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. His real name is Guduru Venkata Satya Krishna Reddy but he is more popular with the names Samrat Reddy and Sam.

He did his schooling from St. Patrick’s High School, Secunderabad, Telangana. He is a graduate in B.A. in Film Making and Television Production.

Physical Appearance

He is a stunning and dashing guy who is approximately 6’ tall and weighs around 80 kg. He has a fit and strong body; chest is to be around 42 inches and biceps are 14 inches. He has dark brown eyes, black hair. He is widely followed and has become a trendsetter for South Indians.

Career

After completing his graduation, he started his acting career in 2000. from the movie ‘Yuvakudu’.

Yuvakudu (2000)

After that, he has appeared in many Telugu films like ‘Panchakshari’, Malini & Co, ‘W/o Ram’, ‘Aha Na Pellanta’, ‘Konchem Ishtam Konchem Kashtam’, etc.

In 2018, he entered the house of ‘Bigg Boss Telugu 2’ as a celebrity contestant.
