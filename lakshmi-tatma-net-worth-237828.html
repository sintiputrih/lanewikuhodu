About

Born with two sets of arms and legs, this Indian child made international headlines as a result of her unusual condition. Surgery to correct her disability was provided at no cost to her family by doctors at Sparsh Hospital in Bangalore, India.

Before Fame

Her multiple limbs resulted from the fact that she had developed in utero as the only surviving member of a pair of conjoined twins (the other fetus' chest and cranium had atrophied in the womb).

Trivia
