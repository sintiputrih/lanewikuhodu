Cardi B Had Boob Job After Giving Birth to Daughter Kulture

New mom, new look! Cardi B had plastic surgery following the arrival of her now 9-month-old daughter, Kulture.

“I just got my boobs redone,” the rapper, 26, told Entertainment Tonight on Wednesday, May 1. “I feel good, but sometimes I feel, like, not, you know? [When] your skin is stretched out. Yes, my daughter f–ked me up. She did, she so did.”

The Hustlers actress, who shares her baby girl with her husband, Offset, also opened up about the mom guilt she experiences while balancing motherhood with her hugely successful career.

“It makes me sad sometimes because it’s like, ‘Oh my gosh, all that traveling,'” Cardi told the outlet. ”I know her sleeping schedule is getting a little messed up … Every single time that she gets on an airplane, it’s all good until the landing. She starts crying, and I just be feeling so sad because I know the pain, you know, when your ears pop.”

She added: “Sometimes I wake up in the morning and she’s in my bed, and I just want to cuddle and [I can’t]. It is getting harder because she knows me. She recognizes more now and … you can just tell she wants me to be there. Sometimes, when she sees me leaving, she looks up and goes, ‘Bitch, where you going?’”

Cardi may travel a lot, but the Bronx native was with Kulture for one major moment in particular. After taking home her first Grammy, she documented her daughter saying, “Mama.”
