Who Is Lucian On Morbius? Marvel Character Guide &amp; Comics Details

Morbius’ antagonist Lucien/Milo isn’t a direct descendant of Marvel Comics, however, the character has a long and illustrious history in the comic book industry. Let us delve into the article to know more about Lucian and Marvel’s Character guide.

When Morbius and Milo first met as children, Morbius renamed Milo Lucien since he had the same blood disease as him. They formed a close kinship as brothers because of their shared condition.

When Lucian was a child, Dr. Michael Morbius met him at a children’s medical center. Milo eventually develops superhuman abilities, but instead of attempting to save people, Milo chooses a more sinister path, serving as the principal antagonist for Morbius and his followers.

Who Is Lucian On Morbius?

Lucien, often known as Milo, is the childhood buddy of the main character, Dr. Michael Morbius. Both characters are affected by the same uncommon and debilitating blood disorder, which Dr. Morbius has dedicated his life to cure.

The remedy comes in the form of scientific vampirism, which Dr. Morbius finds and then infects himself with. Despite his desire to cure his best friend, Morbius believes the treatment to be too risky to accept, denying it to Milo and generating a wedge between them.

Milo, on the other hand, has not given up on his mission to free himself of the sickness. Milo, unknown to Morbius, accepts the treatment anyway, obtaining the unusual powers and bloodlust that his childhood friend had. Milo, on the other hand, relishes the monster he’s become, haunting the night as a blood-drinking, sonar-equipped, superstrong angel of death.

Marvel Character Guide & Comics Details

The cast of Morbius is small, yet many of the performers are well-known to fans.  Morbius was finally released in theaters after a number of delays.

After Venom and Venom: Let There Be Carnage, Morbius is the third installment in Sony’s Spider-Man Universe. With the genesis narrative of Marvel’s “living vampire” like its subject, Morbius is directed and written by Daniel Espinosa from a script by Matt Sazama and Burk Sharpless.

Morbius is played by Jared Leto, who is most recognized for his role as the Joker in David Ayer’s Suicide Squad. Mr. Smith is most recognized for his portrayal of the 11th Doctor on Doctor Who, plays the role of Milo.

Known for her role in Pacific Rim: Uprising, Adria Arjona portrays Martine. Tyrese Gibson portrays Simon, Al Madrigal portrays Alberto “Al” Rodriguez, Jared Harris portrays Dr. Nicholas, and Joseph Esson portrays Milo in the supporting role.
