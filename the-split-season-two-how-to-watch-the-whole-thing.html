The Split season 2: how and where you can watch the entire series right now

The Split season 2 has returned to BBC

    The BBC has brought back its popular legal drama The Split for the second series and viewers are already hooked. The drama, starring Nicola Walker, Stephen Mangan and Donna Air, consists of six episodes centred on a family of lawyers and their professional and personal lives. If you're loving but don't want to wait each week to watch, then you're in luck. You can watch all of the episodes in one sitting!

    WATCH: The official trailer for The Split series two

    On BBC iPlayer, the whole of series two is available to watch right now if you're wanting to binge-watch the show. And if you're new to the drama and want to catch up on past events, then fear not as the whole of series one is also available on iPlayer, so you won't miss a thing.

    MORE: The Split Season 2: Meet the cast of the BBC legal drama

    Hannah and Nathan's marriage is tested once again in the second series

    season two sees family lawyer Hannah Stern return as one of the senior partners in the Noble Hale Defoe law firm. After the first series focused on the breakdown of her marriage to Nathan, played by Stephen Mangan, series two once again sees their marriage tested as Hannah embarks on an affair with her colleague and former lover Christie, played by Barry Atsma. But will Hannah stick to her marriage, or pursue new love? You'll have to watch to find out!

    The drama also focuses on Hannah and Christie's relationship

    One of the stars, Stephen Mangan, took to Twitter to praise the BBC for releasing all the episodes on iPlayer. The actor, who has also starred in comedy shows such as and , wrote: "Never been in a show that's dropped all in one go before. Fascinating to watch the staggered response as people binge on #TheSplit. Thank you for all yr [sic] kind messages. Loved making it." Many replied to Stephen's tweet praising his performance and the programme. One user wrote: "I loved the first series but this one beat it, surely there is another series in it. You were all brilliant but so sad at the end." While another said: "Had to binge watch #TheSplit Excellent all round. Thank you. Can't wait for series 3 now."
