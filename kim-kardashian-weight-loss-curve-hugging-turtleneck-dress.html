Kim Kardashian Flaunts Weight Loss in Tight Turtleneck Dress

Kim Kardashian West has made no secret of her struggles to lose the baby weight that she gained while pregnant with 7-month-old son Saint and her daughter North, 3. After months of grueling workouts and a strict adherence to the Atkins diet helped her shed an incredible 70 pounds, the reality star is showing off her hard work and proudly flaunting her post-baby slim-down.

The mom-of-two stepped out in Los Angeles on Friday in a skintight gray turtleneck number that showed off the results of her significant weight loss and her newly toned-up figure. The Keeping Up with the Kardashians star paired her curvaceous ensemble with gold statement hoops, a sparkly gold clutch, and furry gray d'Orsay-style sandals that highlighted her fit legs.

Kardashian West has been embracing a more natural look recently, and the brunette beauty opted to highlight her features with a bronzed makeup and light contour for the day out. The 35-year-old also swept her hair up in the cute messy bun she's been rocking the past few outings (she recently confirmed on Twitter that this is her hair sans extensions!)

Keep up the hard work, Kim!
