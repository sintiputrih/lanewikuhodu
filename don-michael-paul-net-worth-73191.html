Don Michael Paul Net Worth

Don Michael Paul is an actor, director, and writer born in Newport Beach, California on April 17, 1963. He attended California State University, Fullerton and graduated with a Bachelor's Degree in Theatre Arts. His first script, "Harley Davidson & The Marlboro Man," was sold and cast with Mickey Rourke and Don Johnson. This success led to a four-picture deal at MGM, where he wrote and directed several films. He has since written for many production companies and directed twelve movies, including "Tremors: Bloodlines" and "Death Race" for Universal 1440.
