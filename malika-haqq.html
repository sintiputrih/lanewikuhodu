Malika Haqq is a famous American actress, television personality, model, and director. Malika Haqq is best known for her role as ‘Penny’ in the 2005 film ‘Sky High‘.

Early Life and Childhood

Malika Haqq was born on 10th March 1983. Her birth name is Malika Anjail Haqq. She hails from Los Angeles, California, USA.

Although her nationality is American, she is of African-American ethnicity. Currently, she is 40 years old and her birth sign is Pisces.

She has a twin sister named Khadijah Haqq McCray and a brother named Jamal. They also had older twin sisters but sadly, one of them died at the age of 18.

She has not revealed any information about her parents, childhood, and educational background.

Likewise, she also played the main role in Omarion’s video ‘Speedin’. Later, in 2018, she participated in series 21 of Celebrity Big Brother UK in which she was evicted on Day 29.
