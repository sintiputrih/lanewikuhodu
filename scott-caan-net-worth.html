Scott Caan Net Worth

Scott Caan net worth:

$16 million

Scott Caan is an American actor. His father is actor James Caan. His Hollywood career started in the mid ’90s. He made his debut in the film A Boy Called Hate. He later appeared in the films Enemy of the State and Varsity Blues. He became more prolific in the movies in the 2000s with roles in American Outlaws and Ocean’s Eleven, among others. His role as Turk Malloy in Ocean’s Eleven was reprised in Ocean’s Twelve and Ocean’s Thirteen. He ventured into television and played Scott Lavin on Entourage. Since 2010, he’s been playing Danny “Danno” Williams on Hawaii Five-0.
