Ben Ashkenazy Net Worth: $4 Billion

Ben Ashkenazy is an Israeli American real estate developer and founder, CEO, and majority owner of Ashkenazy Acquisition Corporation. They also hold a $70 million stake in New York’s Plaza Hotel. Ashkenazy purchased his first property at 17 and founded his company in 1987.
