What Happened To The Earl David Reed Show? Rizzo and Jeff Is Replacing Him

The Earl David Reed Show airing on WAIO Radio 95.1 Rochester NY afternoon hit program host been replaced by Jeff and Rizzo. Why?

World best-known touring comedian Earl David Reed is known for his work in radio station 95.1 for afternoons called Earl David Reed Show since August 2020.

As stated by Democrat and Chronicle, The Earl David Reed Show debuted in August 2020 as The Big Show on the station has been canceled following the joining of Rizzo and Jeff. WAOI Fm has ended the Earl David Reed show.

What Happened To The Earl David Reed Show?

After hiring the new host Jeff and Rizzo in the 95.1 programs, Earl David Reed Show has ended. Before that, Reed has served at WPLR New Haven, KBUL and KURR Salt Lake City, and WCMF Rochester.

The most renowned comedian Earl is highly active on Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter. You can follow him on these platforms to know his upcoming events.

His Twitter bio described he celebrated his birthday on March 12 every year. Unfortunately, his actual age is still a secret.

Jeff And Rizzo, Two Host Replaced Earl David Reed Show Announced On Monday

Jeff And Rizzo have replaced the Earl David Reed Show on WAIO Radio 95.1Rochester NY afternoons from 2 to 6 p.m.

Whatsoever, the two hosts, Rizzo and Jeff, have given hints to the public about joining a new radio station in the first week of February through their Facebook posts.

In the post, they wrote by sharing their images, “WE GOT A JOB!. Anyone wanna guess where? What kind of station? Love to hear what you think? Can’t say anything else yet. But you all have your boys back. We MISSED you. Hint: It’s in North America.”

Who was the new afternoon host on 95.1 FM Rizzo and Jeff? Rich ‘Rizzo’ Deaver and Jeff Daly have been appointed as the hosts for “Radio 95.1” WAIO Honeoye Falls/Rochester NY, which airs on weekdays from 2 to 6 p.m.

According to the reports of Radio Insight, they previously hosted a morning show at MacDonald Garber Broadcasting CHR “106 KHQ” WKHQ-FM Charlevoix/Traverse City Michigan until early 2020.

The duo worked together at CHR “98.7 Amp Radio” WDZH Detroit, where Daly co-hosted mornings and Deaver was the producer.
