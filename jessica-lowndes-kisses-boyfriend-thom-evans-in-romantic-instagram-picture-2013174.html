Jessica Lowndes Kisses Boyfriend Thom Evans in Romantic Instagram Picture

Pucker up! Less than a month after Olympic skier Jeremy Bloom confirmed to Us Weekly that his one-year romance with Jessica Lowndes had ended, the 90210 actress reunited with another ex: Thom Evans, a Scottish rugby player and model. The brunette beauty shared a handful of pictures of herself with the athlete via Instagram — including one of the two sharing a sweet kiss — while attending the Coachella Music and Arts Festival in Indio, Calif., over the weekend.

“Good people, good music, good times,” Lowndes, 24, wrote in one caption. Evans, 28, also shared a PDA picture via his own Instagram account. (The two were briefly linked in June 2010.)

When the on-again loves hit up the The Hard Rock Music Lounge April 12, a source tells Us Lowndes and Evans “were all over each other. They couldn’t stop kissing and showing PDA in front of everyone.”
