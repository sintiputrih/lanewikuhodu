Wiki/Biography

Prabhu was born on Tuesday, 3 April 1973 (age 46 years; as in 2019), in Mysore, Karnataka. His zodiac sign is Aries. He got training in Bharatanatyam (an Indian classical dance form) from, Dharmaraj and Udupi Lakshminarayanan. He dropped out of his school and assisted his father in dance choreography in the South-Indian films.

Prabhu was born into a Lingayat Hindu Family. [1] His father, Mugur Sundar, has worked as a choreographer in South-Indian films. His mother’s name is Mahadevamma. Prabhu has two brothers, Raju Sundaram and Nagendra Prasad, both are choreographers.

Career

Prabhu’s father is a well-known choreographer of South-Indian films. Prabhu got inspiration to work as a choreographer from his father, so, he started assisting his father and accompanied him on the sets of films. He started his career as a choreographer in Tamil films with Vetri Vizha (1989). He has worked as a dance choreographer in more than 100 Hindi and regional films before making an appearance on the big screen. Some of his popular choreographed songs are ‘Muqabala Muqabala’ (Hum Se Hai Muqabla, 1994), ‘Urvashi Urvashi’ (Hum Se Hai Muqabla, 1994) ‘Kay Sera Sera’ (Pukar, 2000).

He has acted in many Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, Malayalam, and Kannada films. Some of his films as an actor are ABCD (2013), Lakshmi (2018), Pon Manickavel (2019), and Street Dancer (2020).

He has tried his hands on the direction as well, some of his directorial films are Shankardada Zindabad (2007), Wanted (2009), Ramaiya Vastavaiya (2013), Dabangg 3 (2019), and Radhe (2020).
